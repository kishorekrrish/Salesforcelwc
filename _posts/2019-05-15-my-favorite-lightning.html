My favorite Lightning Platform/Experience features in Summer'19 release



As expected Summer'19 release is here. Lightning enthusiasts, here are the exciting features of this release.
Source : Salesforce Summer'19 Release Notes


Lightning Platform

  • Lightning Web Components: Container Expansion
  • Lightning Flow: Apex Building Blocks with Types


Lightning Experience

  • Record Page: Full View
  • Related List Enhancements
  • Related List Filters
  • In-App Guidance






Lightning Web Components: Container Expansion

Implement Lightning Web Components and the latest web standards in more places than ever before, including Visualforce, Lightning Out, email integrations, and stand-alone apps.

for more details: click here

Lightning Flow: Apex Building Blocks with Types

Turn Apex classes and external data into reusable, customizable components for app development with point-and-click tools such as Flow and Strategy Builder.

for more details: click here






Record Page: Full View

Get a single, data-dense view of your records, including details, related list hovers, full 10-column tables, and even custom lists and activity timelines for greater context.

for more details: click here


Related List Enhancements

Now you can see, sort, and resize columns in related lists. Even wrap text and take mass actions from a related list itself.

for more details: click here

Related List Filters

Add filters to any field on a related list to easily find additional, relevant data.

for more details: click here

In-App Guidance

Help your entire organization get up and running in Lightning Experience. Create custom in-app prompts, introduce new features, and view stats on how users interacted with your prompts.

for more details: click here
